About me
“I have always loved capturing the moments of life. So, my sister suggested that I try my hand at professional photography. I bought a small DSLR Canon and started shooting. I fell in love with the profession and adopted the mantra “It's not just a photo but an experience!” I wanted to use my abilities to then create opportunities for young people to have affordable access to professional grade headshots, events shots, and portraiture.”
